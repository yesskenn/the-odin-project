Enchilada Casserole

A bite of a cheesy enchilada casserole.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Cheese
  • tortillas
  • Las Palmas enchilada sauce (or you can make your own!).
  • Black or kidney beans, canned
  • Spanish Rice
  • Corn
  • Serrano peppers
  • Other optional ingredients: Chicken, onions, avocado, sour cream, lime, cilantro.

Steps

  1. If you are using chicken, start by cooking the chicken to your liking.
  2. While the chicken is cooking, dice the onions and slice the serrano peppers.
  3. Throw in some onions and peppers into a saucepan with some olive oil. Add beans and corn. Heat on low, and cover for 7-9 minutes.
  4. Cook spanish rice accoording to instructions, it should be about 20 minutes.
  5. Once all your ingredients are prepped, you can begin your layering.
  6. Take some souce and pour it over the casserole pan. Grab some tortillas and place them in the pan, pour moroe sauce over them.
  7. The first layer is rice, then beans, onion, then cheese, more sauce, tortillas and so forth. Be liberal with the cheese.
  8. Bake in oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees.
