Air Fryer Meatloaf

Meatloaf, made in an airfryer.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Egg, lightly beaten
  • Cooked bacon
  • Worcestershire sauce, ketchup
  • 3 tbsp dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 thickly-sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F.
  2. Combine beef, onion, bread crumbs, thyme, bacon, sauce, ketchup, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Knead and mix thoroughly.
  3. Transfer to small loaf pan and press in mushrooms. Coat with olive oil.
  4. Roast meatloaf in airfryer until nicely browned, about 25 minutes.
  5. Let meatloaf rest at least 10 minutes, then slice and serve.
