Avocado Salmon Toast

Classic millenial avocado salmon toast.

Ingredients

  • Your fave bread
  • Everything Smoked Salmon from Trader Joe's
  • Avocado
  • Jalapeños
  • Lemon
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. While the bread toasts - slice the avocado, place it in a small bowl, and mush. Add salt, pepper, and lemon. I like to add serrano peppers for an extra kick.
  2. Butter the toast, spread avocado on top.
  3. Layer slices of salmon on top and add jalapeños (or serranos) to taset.
  4. Sprinkle with dill and sesame seeds.
